Clase Azul Reposado: A Smooth and Sophisticated Tequila

Clase Azul Reposado is a premium tequila brand that has gained worldwide recognition for its exceptional quality and craftsmanship. The brand was founded in 1997 by Arturo Lomeli, a Mexican entrepreneur with a passion for tequila. Clase Azul Reposado is known for its smooth and sophisticated flavor profile, which is achieved through a meticulous distillation process and aging in oak barrels.

Clase Azul Reposado is made from 100% blue Weber agave, which is grown in the highlands of Jalisco, Mexico. The agave plants are carefully selected and harvested by hand, ensuring that only the best quality agave is used in the production process. The Distillation Process: Crafting a Smooth and Sophisticated Flavor Profile

The distillation process of Clase Azul Reposado is what sets it apart from other tequilas on the market. After the agave hearts are cooked, they are crushed to extract the juice, which is then fermented using a proprietary yeast strain. This fermentation process takes place in small stainless steel tanks, allowing for precise control over temperature and fermentation time.

Once the fermentation is complete, the liquid is double distilled in copper pot stills. This traditional method of distillation helps to remove impurities and create a smooth and refined spirit. The resulting tequila is then aged in oak barrels for a minimum of eight months, allowing it to develop its unique flavor profile.

Aging in Oak Barrels: Enhancing the Aroma and Taste of Clase Azul Reposado

The aging process is a crucial step in the production of Clase Azul Reposado. The tequila is aged in oak barrels, which impart rich flavors and aromas to the spirit. Clase Azul uses a combination of new and used American oak barrels, as well as French oak barrels, to achieve the desired flavor profile.

The type of oak used and the length of aging can greatly influence the final product. The oak barrels add notes of vanilla, caramel, and spice to the tequila, creating a complex and well-rounded flavor profile. The aging process also allows the tequila to mellow and become smoother over time.

Tasting Notes: Describing the Flavor Profile of Clase Azul Reposado

Clase Azul Reposado is known for its rich and complex flavor profile. On the nose, it offers aromas of vanilla, caramel, and toasted oak, with hints of citrus and agave. The palate is smooth and balanced, with flavors of sweet agave, caramel, and baking spices. The finish is long and lingering, with a touch of spice and a hint of sweetness.

The tequila has a velvety texture and a well-rounded mouthfeel. It is incredibly smooth and easy to drink, making it a favorite among tequila enthusiasts. The flavors are perfectly balanced, with just the right amount of sweetness and spice.
